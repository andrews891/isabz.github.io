The Applications of Computer Science

Source: Unsplash

What is computer science?

Computer science is the study of computation. There is a wide variety of applications in many different fields, such as AI, networks, cybersecurity and databases. Computer science is used to solve problems and improve our quality of life.

What are some of the applications?

  • Education: As you may have noticed, education in these recent times would be near impossible without computer science.
  • Medicine: Brilliant developments have been made in medicine with the help of computer science.
  • Safety: Did you know computer science has saved lives?
  • Not to mention computer science's contributions to art, communication, other sciences, and more
